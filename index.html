Why This Contest Exists

Celebrating the in-between.

Growing up multicultural means carrying more than one story. Blend In Stand Out invites students to put that experience into art — and be seen for exactly who they are.

🎨

Express Your Identity

Use any art form to explore what it means to grow up between cultures — the beauty, the complexity, and everything in between.

🌍

Be Seen & Celebrated

Your story deserves a platform. Winning and featured artwork will be showcased publicly, putting your voice and vision in front of a real audience.

✨

Join a Community

Connect with other students from across the country who share that same feeling of straddling two worlds — and making something beautiful from it.

About the Founder

Started by a student, for students.

Blend In Stand Out was founded by Mia Shrank, a junior at Watchung Hills Regional High School in Warren, NJ. Half Korean, half white — and an artist who has studied in Barcelona and Florence — Mia created this contest to build the community she wished she'd had.

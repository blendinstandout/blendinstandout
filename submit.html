Blend In Stand Out 2026

Submit Your Art

Submissions are open April 8 – May 10, 2026. Free to enter. Any medium.

Before You Submit

Read through the checklist below to make sure your submission is ready. Questions? Email us at blendinstandout.art@gmail.com.

Submission Checklist

  • Your artwork is original and created by you
  • You have a high-quality image of your work (.jpg or .png)
  • You've written a title for your piece
  • You've written a short artist statement (100–300 words)
  • You have your parent or guardian's permission to submit
  • You're in grades 6–12

Accepted Mediums

Painting, drawing, digital art, photography, mixed media, printmaking, collage, and sculpture (submitted as a photograph).

Important Dates

Opens: April 8, 2026

Deadline: May 10, 2026 at 11:59 PM ET

Winners announced: Late May 2026

For Parents & Guardians

A note on permissions.

All submitting students will be asked to confirm that they have their parent or guardian's permission to enter and to have their artwork displayed on the Blend In Stand Out website and social media channels. The submission form includes a required checkbox for this confirmation. We will always credit your student as the artist and will never sell or commercially license submitted artwork.

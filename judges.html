Judging Criteria

What they're looking for.

While we finalize the panel, here's how submissions will be evaluated.

🎨

Artistic Quality (40%)

Craft, technique, and visual impact. Judges look for intention and skill, not perfection. Any medium is valued equally.

🌍

Connection to Theme (40%)

How thoughtfully does the work engage with the multicultural experience?

✍️

Artist Statement (20%)

A short written reflection on what the piece means. Judges value honesty and personal voice over formal writing.
